  • 프리미엄 구독 구매·검증과 관련해 다음 정보가 결제 사업자에서 처리될 수 있습니다.
    • Google Play 결제: 구매 토큰, 주문/거래 식별자, 영수증 정보
    • RevenueCat: 앱/인스턴스 식별자, 구독 상태(활성/만료/기간), 영수증/토큰, 앱/플랫폼 버전 등 검증에 필요한 기술 정보
  • 광고 식별자(AAID) 및 광고/행태추적 SDK, 분석/크래시 SDK를 사용하지 않습니다.

0. Scope

This policy applies to the Moong Counter mobile app and this policy page. Core features (counting, history, themes, sounds) run on-device; we do not provide cloud sync.

1. Data We Collect

  • No sign-up/login; we do not collect direct identifiers (name, email, phone).
  • History & settings are stored locally on your device and are not sent to our servers.
  • For purchases and subscription verification, third-party processors may handle:
    • Google Play Billing: purchase token, order/transaction IDs, receipt info
    • RevenueCat: app/instance IDs, subscription status (active/expired/period), purchase receipts/tokens, app/platform versions and related technical info
  • No ad ID, no advertising/tracking SDKs, no analytics/crash SDKs.
  • No sensitive categories (e.g., health, biometrics).

2. Processors / Third Parties (Summary)

Processor Items Purpose Location / Retention
Google Play Billing Purchase token, order/transaction IDs, receipt info Payment processing & receipt verification; restore/refund; fraud prevention Outside your country (e.g., US) / Transaction records kept 5 years
RevenueCat App/instance IDs, subscription status, receipts/tokens, app/platform versions Server-side subscription management & verification; state sync; fraud prevention Outside your country (e.g., US) / Deleted after purpose or per law/policy

We do not run a user account database and do not collect direct identifiers.

3. Purposes

  • Verify subscriptions and provide premium features
  • Restore purchases; process refunds/cancellations; prevent abuse
  • Minimal technical processing for stability

3-1. Legal Bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

  • Contract performance
  • Legal obligations (tax/transaction retention; refunds/cooling-off)
  • Legitimate interests (fraud prevention, security)
  • Consent where applicable for optional features

4. International Transfers

Data may be processed outside your country (e.g., US). Transfers use appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCC) plus encryption/access controls where needed.

5. Retention & Deletion

  • E-commerce transactions: 5 years
  • Complaints/disputes: 3 years
  • Otherwise deleted without undue delay once the purpose is met

6. Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, deletion, restriction
  • Cancellation/refunds follow Google Play policies
  • Contact us below; we respond within statutory timeframes

For verification, we may request your purchase token or order number. Some requests may be limited by legal retention obligations. You may complain to your supervisory authority (e.g., EU DPA/UK ICO).

7. Security

  • HTTPS in transit; data minimization; access controls
  • Access logs and internal management measures

8. App Permissions

  • Draw over other apps: floating counter (with user consent)
  • Vibration: haptic feedback (optional)
  • Notifications: system foreground status notice (no marketing pushes)
  • Foreground service: keep the overlay running; persistent notification

If not granted, certain features (e.g., overlay) are limited.

Not directed to children (under 13 in the US; under 14 in Korea; higher local thresholds apply). We delete any children’s data we become aware of.

We will anonymize or delete RevenueCat records where possible and delete Google Play transaction records after the statutory period.

Third-party sites/services are governed by their own policies.

No automated decision-making or profiling producing legal/similar effects.

If a personal data incident is confirmed, we will notify and take protective measures as required by law.

15. Changes

Premium features via a monthly auto-renewing subscription. Prices vary by region/exchange/platform; final price is shown before purchase.
